About Us

Kasale Engineering was founded back in the early 2000s at Plot # in Livingstone Zambia. It was aimed at designing, making and re-engineering old furnitures to make it as new as ever. Our goal has always been the same- to make good, quality and long lasting furnitures for our customers at really low prices. Our vision is see every infrastructure- house, church, shop, school, office etc with the best furniture. We are also specialized in repairing vehicles. We are situation along mandela road, at plot # 2339, Livingstone, Zambia.
